Town Council Approves New Riverside Park Expansion After Years of Debate

By Margaret Holloway  ·  Staff Reporter  ·  March 3, 2026

After more than three years of community discussions, public hearings, and a few heated town hall meetings, the Maplewood County Town Council voted 5–2 on Monday night to approve the long-anticipated expansion of Riverside Park. The project, which carries a budget of $1.4 million, will add walking trails, a new pavilion, and a restored wetlands area along the eastern bank.
